Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2025

Markly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Authentication credentials

User Content

We store the content you create within the app:

  • Text notes and ideas
  • Boards and collections
  • Tags and organization data

Audio Recordings

Our app uses the microphone permission (RECORD_AUDIO) to enable voice input features. When you use voice recording:

  • Audio is recorded only when you explicitly tap the record button
  • Recordings are processed to convert speech to text
  • Audio files are stored securely and associated with your account
  • You can delete your recordings at any time

We do not record audio in the background or without your knowledge.

Third-Party Integrations

If you connect third-party services (Notion, Todoist), we store:

  • OAuth tokens to maintain your connection
  • Workspace/project identifiers you select

We do not access or store your data from these services beyond what's necessary for syncing.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • App performance metrics
  • Error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Markly service
  • Process voice recordings into text for your notes
  • Sync your content with connected third-party services
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Process subscription payments

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase)
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), payment processing (RevenueCat/Apple/Google)
  • Third-Party Integrations: Only when you explicitly connect services like Notion or Todoist
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Disconnect third-party integrations at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days

Children's Privacy

Markly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
